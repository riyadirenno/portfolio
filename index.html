About Me

I was born and raised in Jakarta, Indonesia. I’m currently in Ireland to pursue a degree in Communications in Creative Media at Dundalk Institute of Ireland.

My workflow revolves around Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Sketch and InVision. I’m fairly competent with HTML and CSS and am familiar with some PHP.

I’m also fairly comfortable with Adobe Premiere Pro for light video work.

I'm highly flexible and am motivated to learn from whoever is willing and is always open for taking criticism. I always try to find ways to improve myself by challenging myself through exploring new opportunities
