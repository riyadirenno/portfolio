We are Less than 3.

Less than 3 is a dating platform which focuses on your interest and does nothing but help you find your perfect match. Swiper no more Swiping.

We’re a young start-up currently based in Dundalk on a mission to connect people from all around Ireland to one another in the name of pure love.

We are Less than 3.

Less than 3 is formed by four Dundalk Institute Technology graduates with the same goal; to help people find their perfect match. Formed in 2017, Less than 3 has removed swiping on the dating culture and focus’ on things that really matters: Honesty, Positivity and Integrity. We here at Less than 3 come in to the office every week to tackle challenges daily and help all our users as much as we can to find their perfect match.
