    After much speculation in regards to their debut ever since the reality TV show Produce IOI, PRISTIN; formerly known as Pledis Girls has officially debuted. 'WEE WOO' is the title track for their debut album 'Hi! PRISTIN'.

    Months after connecting over Twitter, Wale and BTS' Rap Monster teamed up to release their latest track 'Change'. 'Change' is one of the very few tracks out there which involves an international artist and a K-Pop artist.

    Ever since their contract with Cube Entertainment expired, the boys decided not to renew their contracts; instead making their own label called Around US Entertainment. Now promoting under the name Highlight, they've recently released their new track titled 'Plz Don't be Sad'.

    MONSTA X is another up and coming group from the very famous Starship Entertainment. 'All In' is their title track from their album The Clan Pt.1 which is released back in May 2016.

    Everyday Girls Day. After 21 months hiatus as a group, Girls Day finally announced their comeback and released their latest album titled Everyday #5.

    After dropping many teasers about their upcoming collaborations on social media, Cao Lu, Kisum and Yerin has finally released their track titled 'Spring Again'. The track perfectly embodies the spring mood featuring vocals from the three girls with a sweet guitar acoustic accompaniment.

    CNBLUE has done it again. After pioneering the Idol-Band movement in the industry, they've done an 180; straying away from its soft rock leanings to a track featuring electric elements. 'Between Us' is a breathe of fresh air from all the released tracks in charts right now.
