Trouble of All Kinds

Band identity. Album artwork and mock-up website design creation.

View the Album Artwork View the Website Design

  • RESOURCES USED
  • Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

  • TIMEFRAME
  • 2 Months

This project showcases the creation of an album artwork and lyrics page for a fictional band. We were given total freedom as to the color schemes and the genre of music we are going for. Following the completion of the latter, we were tasked to create a parralax one page web mockup which compliments the design we’ve completed before.

The name of the band and the album was given to us randomly. Through the process I’ve received “The Greatest Question” as my band name and “Trouble of All Kinds” as my album name. The genre I’m going for my artist is Indie. I don’t listen to much Indie myself but I think the name “The Greatest Question” fits the bill.

I choose Purple and Green as the two primary colors for my design as I believe that the two colors compliment each other just well. I decided to stick by just using these two colors as I believe adding more would make the overall piece lose focus.

The whole project took over 8 weeks to complete. We were given 4 weeks to complete our album artwork and lyrics page, and another 4 weeks to finish the mockup parallax design. The first two weeks of each of project is filled with sketching and creating different mockups. By week 3 the class would conduct a class feedback session where everyone in the class would give their personal feedback on the design and advise me on what I should do to improve the design. By week 4 I would be completing the final design based on the feedback I received during the feedback session.
