Renno Riyadi Identity

Branding yourself. Logo creation and an implementation of a Style Guide. Personal Website.

This project showcases the creation of a logo, a style guide which compliments the logo and a website showcasing the whole brand. For this assignment we were tasked to make a logo for ourselves and a style guide. After the project is finished, during Summer 2017 I decided to pick up on the project and decided to make a website with it.

The logo is simply my handwriting traced. Though simple I find that it distributes multiple messages which expresses my personality. The colors I incorporate with the design is basically my favorite colors compiled to one, though I take not to whether the colors compliment each other or not.

The whole project took over 1 month to complete. We were given 3 weeks to complete both our logo and style guide. Through the 3 weeks, the first week is done through sketching and research. By the second week I’ve started tracing the final design in Illustrator and began working on the style guide. By the last week, I would be completing the final design for submission.

The website took approx. 1 week to make. I did research and make wireframes on how I want the website to look and feel. From paper I decided to replicate the design through Sketch and after everything is finalised I started coding.

Different from Experience Hallyu, this is a personal project hence I don’t really have due dates in which I have to meet hence I decided to take my time a little bit. I decided to pick on CSS preprocessors as whilst researching I find out that by using it, it would make my workflow much easier. It also allows me to code much faster and be more in control of the whole thing. I picked up SCSS whilst coding the website.
