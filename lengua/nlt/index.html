About NLT Limo

NLT Limo is dedicated to providing our clients with excellent limousine services coupled with a personal touch. Here at NLT Limo, we focus on delivering exceptional and outstanding service for any occasion.

Our company specializes in providing transport services in Singapore and Malaysia. From large to small parties, our services have been specifically tailored to meet each and every of your expectations, within a corporate or personal context.

We own a large fleet of new and luxurious Limo selection that are well-maintained and in pristine condition. Our team of chauffeurs are dedicated and competent and will go the extra mile in serving you.
