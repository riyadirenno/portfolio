Project Overview

BLNKDISRUPTION is a project which aims to investigate the evolution of creative media, what it is in its traditional form and what it can develop into with the assistance of modern technologies. The project aims to make people aware of the future possibilities of creative media and will explore the potential of AR technologies as a future possibility of an art medium in public spaces. BLNKDISRUPTION was awarded the best project in the annual FIS Graduate Exhibition in 2019.

Discussion

My responsibilities in this project include designing and developing the project website, oversee the overall style guide of the project, and assist the primary graphic designer on their work. I also oversee the development of the animation of the project and implementing our artwork into the AR platform.

Key Takeaways

Here are some of the main takeaways from working in this project:

  • Communication between each member is key. Do not hesitate to say something when there is something concerning you and let your voice be heard whenever you have a problem.
  • Put trust in your group members. You are not there to take all the burden of the group, delegate tasks and always be transparent to your fellow members.
  • Always have a back-up plan! When we found out our initial AR platform will be discontinued later on that year, we decided to take a step back and look up other alternatives, which leads us to use Lens Studio.
  • Hard work does pay off. We were awarded the best project in the annual FIS Graduate Exhibition in 2019! üèÜ
